Authentic Masala Chai

There is nothing quite like a steaming cup of Masala Chai to start your morning. The key is in boiling the spices *with* the water.

View Full Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 cup Whole Milk
  • 2 tsp Black Tea Leaves
  • 4 Green Cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 inch Ginger, grated

Instructions

  1. Boil water with crushed cardamom and ginger for 3 minutes.
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2 minutes using low heat.
  3. Add milk and bring to a boil again.
  4. Strain and serve hot with biscuits.

Classic Butter Chicken

Rich, creamy, and mildly spicy. This restaurant-style butter chicken is easier to make at home than you think.

"Tip: Always use Fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi) at the end. It gives the dish that signature smoky aroma."
View Full Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken Thighs
  • 2 tbsp Butter/Ghee
  • 1 cup Tomato Puree
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 tbsp Cashew Paste
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
  2. Sear chicken in a pan until golden. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook tomato puree, cashew paste, and spices.
  4. Add chicken back, pour in cream, and simmer.
  5. Finish with fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi).
